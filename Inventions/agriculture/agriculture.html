Agriculture

Agriculture has been a cornerstone of human civilization, and innovations in this field have continually shaped how we grow, harvest, and distribute food. From the early use of simple tools and irrigation systems to the development of modern machinery, genetic engineering, and sustainable farming practices, advancements in agriculture have greatly increased food production and efficiency. These innovations not only support global food security but also help address environmental challenges and adapt to changing climates. The breakthroughs featured below represent key milestones in agricultural technology that have transformed the way we cultivate the land and feed the world.
