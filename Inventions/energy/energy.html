Energy

Energy innovation has been a driving force behind human progress, powering everything from homes and industries to transportation and communication. From the harnessing of fire and fossil fuels to the rise of renewable sources like solar, wind, and hydropower, advancements in energy technology have shaped the modern world. These innovations not only support economic growth and improve living standards but also play a vital role in addressing climate change and building a more sustainable future.
