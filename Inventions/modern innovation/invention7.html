Short Description

Augmented Reality (AR) in tourism integrates digital content into real-world environments, offering immersive experiences that enhance how travelers explore and interact with destinations.

Purpose

The primary goal of AR in tourism is to enrich travel experiences by providing interactive, personalized, and informative content, thereby increasing customer engagement and satisfaction.

How It Is Made

AR applications are developed using technologies like 3D modeling, geolocation, and real-time data integration. These apps overlay digital information such as historical facts, navigation aids, or virtual reconstructions onto physical surroundings, accessible via smartphones, tablets, or AR glasses.

When It Is Made

The implementation of AR in tourism has gained momentum over the past decade, with significant advancements in mobile technology and increased demand for interactive travel experiences.

Who Made It

Companies like Rock Paper Reality specialize in creating AR solutions for the tourism industry, collaborating with travel operators, museums, and cultural institutions to develop tailored AR experiences.

Contribution To Society

AR in tourism enhances cultural understanding and accessibility by bringing historical sites and stories to life. It supports the tourism sector by offering innovative ways to attract visitors, improve services, and generate new revenue streams.
