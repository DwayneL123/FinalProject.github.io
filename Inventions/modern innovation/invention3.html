Blockchain

Short Description

Layer 1 and Layer 2 refer to different types of blockchain scaling solutions: Layer 1 improves the base blockchain protocol, while Layer 2 builds on top of it to enhance speed and efficiency.

Purpose

The purpose of these scaling solutions is to address the limitations of blockchain networks, particularly transaction speed and cost, to support broader adoption and usability.

How It Is Made

Layer 1 solutions involve direct changes to the blockchain's core protocol (e.g., increasing block size, changing consensus mechanisms).
Layer 2 solutions operate off-chain and interact with Layer 1 to finalize transactions, often using techniques like rollups, sidechains, or state channels. .

When It Is Made

Layer 1 and Layer 2 innovations have emerged over the past decade, especially as blockchain usage grew and scalability became a critical issue (notably post-2017 during high Ethereum traffic).

Who Made It

These solutions have been developed by blockchain communities, research teams, and developers from projects like Ethereum, Bitcoin Lightning Network, Optimism, and Arbitrum.

Contribution To Society

They enhance blockchain technology by reducing transaction costs, increasing throughput, and making decentralized applications (dApps) more viable, thus enabling greater innovation in finance, supply chains, gaming, and more.
